About Coralie

Coralie is a vibrant young mind with a big imagination. She’s always ready to explore new projects—whether that’s crafting, gaming, or painting her nails in every color under the sun. When she’s not in front of a screen building epic Minecraft worlds, she’s likely testing fresh nail art techniques or learning about self-expression through art.

From a young age, she’s been drawn to helping friends feel better when they’re down, which is why she’s exploring how Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) and Art Therapy can help people express themselves and heal.

Future Goals

Aspiring DBT/Art Therapist

Coralie’s fascination with art goes beyond just nails and creative projects. She believes art can be a powerful way for people to express themselves, especially when words alone aren’t enough. That’s why she’s learning about Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) techniques and how combining them with art can help individuals manage emotions, practice mindfulness, and spark healing.
